Destinations

We’re going to Krabi province in southern Thailand!

Specifically, my idea is to stay at and explore the island of Koh Lanta, which is beautiful but not as touristy and commercialized as other areas such as Ao Nang. It’s the right place for diving, relaxing on the beach, and enjoying nature. See a picture!

Since we’ll be in Thailand for 5 days, we can also take day trips to Koh Phi Phi. More populated and popular than Koh Lanta, there’s lots of entertainment (and crowds) here. See a picture!

Another island to visit is Rai Leh, known for towering limestone karsts and excellent rock climbing. See a picture!

Travel requirements

Visa

For US, Hong Kong, Netherlands, Germany, Brazil passport holders, no visa is required.

For China passport holders, you can get a visa on arrival (200-400 RMB fee).

Basically, there shouldn’ be any issue with visas.

Recommended vaccinations

Routine vaccinations, plus:
- Hepatitis A
- Typhoid

For more health information, see this CDC travel page for Thailand

Other requirements

I think for travelers from Brazil, there may be an additional requirement for health documentation, related to Yellow Fever or something like that… To be confirmed!
