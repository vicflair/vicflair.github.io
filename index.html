Destinations

We’re going to Krabi province in southern Thailand!

Specifically, my idea is to stay at and explore the island of Koh Lanta, which is beautiful but not as touristy and commercialized as other areas such as Ao Nang. It’s the right place for diving, relaxing on the beach, and enjoying nature.

Since we’ll be in Thailand for 5 days, we can also take day trips to Koh Phi Phi. More populated and popular than Koh Lanta, there’s lots of entertainment (and crowds) here.

Another island to visit is Rai Leh, known for towering limestone karsts and excellent rock climbing.

Activities

Some of the things we can enjoy:

  • Beautiful, relaxing beaches, such as Long Beach

  • Beach-side bars and parties until the late evening

  • Discovering the rainforest on a nature safari, including natural hot springs, bamboo rafting, and elephant riding

  • Scuba diving and snorkeling. Explore the underwater world! Example dive trip for beginners

  • Kayaking

  • Windsurfing and paddle boarding

  • Delicious Thai cuisine

  • Rock climbing at Rai Leh

  • Massages

  • Thai cooking lessons

Travel requirements

Visa

Visa exemption for US, Hong Kong, Netherlands, Germany, Brazil passport holders
Visa on arrival (200-400 RMB fee) for China, Taiwan passport holders

Basically, there shouldn’ be any issue with visas.

Recommended vaccinations

Routine vaccinations, plus:
- Hepatitis A
- Typhoid

For more health information, see:
https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/destinations/traveler/none/Thailand

Other requirements

I think for travelers from Brazil, there may be an additional requirement for health documentation, related to Yellow Fever or something like that… To be confirmed!
