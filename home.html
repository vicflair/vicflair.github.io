About me

A data scientist at Booking.com B.V. working on fraud detection throughout the business.

Previously, I was a senior engineer at the MITRE Corporation, working on missile defense sensor systems.

I am skilled in Python, R, MATLAB, Hadoop, Hive, and SQL. I have also dabbled in C++, C#, and Perl.

I am experienced in machine learning, statistics, text mining, and applications thereof.

I have a M.S. in Systems Engineering and B.S. in Electrical Engineering, both from Boston University. I also spent some time pursuing Ph.D in Comptuer Science at the Ecole Polytechnique Federale de Lausanne.
